Ensured Privacy

We do not ask for your email, phone number, or any contact details. Once your class session is over, we delete all the whiteboard workings, video conference recordings, and any other details you provide us during the conference such as your name. We do not share any information of yourself with any third-party clients. We ensure that you are given a secure, private classroom.

Our mission is to allow students and teachers to connect and learn naturally.
We make sure privacy issues are not a hurdle to this.
